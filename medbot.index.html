About MedBot Lens

MedBot Lens is a medicine awareness platform that helps users understand commonly used medicines using verified public drug-label data. It does not provide dosage or treatment advice.

Indian Context

Indian medicines are often known by brand names like Disprin, Saridon, Crocin, or Naxdom. MedBot Lens maps these brands to their generic drugs and provides safety awareness.
Brand mapping is for awareness only. Self-medication is discouraged.
